## Project Overview

This project is created as part of the CSE0001 Digital Literacy course, where I take on the role of a Student Digital Ambassador. The aim is to develop awareness about using digital tools effectively, maintaining a professional online presence, and practicing safe and responsible behavior on the internet.

The repository includes a series of tasks covering infographic creation, digital portfolio setup, coding and collaboration platforms, professional communication, and cybercrime awareness. Each task reflects practical skills that are essential for students in today’s digital environment.

## Task-wise Summary

- **Task 1 – Infographic**  
  Designed a one-page infographic using Canva to spread awareness about digital literacy. The infographic covers key topics such as safe internet practices, useful digital tools for students, professional online presence, and email etiquette in a visually engaging format.

- **Task 2 – Digital Portfolio**  
  Created and updated professional profiles on platforms like GitHub, LinkedIn, ResearchGate, StackOverflow and Kaggle. This task focused on building a strong digital identity, showcasing academic details, and understanding how these platforms can support future career growth.

- **Task 3 – Platforms**  
  Explored coding and collaboration platforms by completing a beginner-level problem on HackerRank and creating a Google Form titled “Digital Literacy Awareness Quiz.” The form was linked to a response sheet for collecting and analyzing data. Link of the Form: https://forms.gle/uyCJQoveCAhq1oZP9

- **Task 4 – Email Etiquette**  
  Drafted two professional emails—one for requesting an assignment extension and another for applying to a summer internship. Also prepared a checklist of do’s and don’ts for responsible social media usage among students.

- **Task 5 – Cybercrime Awareness**  
  Developed a case study on UPI/Online Payment Fraud, explaining how such scams occur, who they target, and their consequences. Additionally, created a prevention checklist with practical tips to promote safe online behavior.
