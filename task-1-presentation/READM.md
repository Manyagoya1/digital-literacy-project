In this task, I created an infographic to raise awareness about digital literacy. The infographic was designed using Canva, an online design tool. It explains the concept of digital literacy and highlights key aspects such as safe internet practices, useful digital tools for students, professional online presence.

The main objective of this infographic is to present essential digital knowledge in a clear and easy-to-understand manner for students. It is designed to help students become more aware of how to use digital platforms effectively and responsibly.
