## Social Media Do’s and Don’ts for College Students

## Do’s
- Think before posting and ensure your content is appropriate
- Keep your profiles professional and respectful
- Verify information before sharing it
- Use privacy settings to control who can see your posts
- Be respectful and polite in comments and message

## Don'ts
- Don’t share personal or sensitive information publicly
- Don’t post offensive, harmful, or inappropriate content
- Don’t spread rumors or unverified information
- Don’t engage in cyberbullying or negative behavior
- Don’t accept friend requests from unknown people
